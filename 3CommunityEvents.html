We love getting together for a good celebration. Come join us at some of these events.

DanceNightAway Celebration

Our community is proud to join hands with the Dance Foundation of NC and celebrate its 50th anniversary!

Ice Cream Social

Do you love ice cream, a nice, cold treat with just a little sugar? If you do, attend this fun social event. Socialize under the afternoon sun and relax.

Community Coffee Hour

For those early workers, every Thursday at 7:30 AM is a community coffee hour. Get a good cup of coffee, hang out with friends before a long day.

Open Mic Night

We all love a good comedy act and a showcase of the talent every person has to offer. Come to Open Mic night, get some popcorn, and watch a good show.

Game Night

Love playing games with friends, whether it's classic board games or energetic outdoor games? Join us at game night at the clubhouse.

Movie & Popcorn Social

Who doesn't love a good kids' movie night? Attend our movie social every Saturday Night. Of course, you can't have a movie without popcorn, which will be provided fresh and free.
